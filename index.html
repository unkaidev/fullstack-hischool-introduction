Fullstack Hi-school

The final result is the creation of a complete website that provides functions to support high schools, facilitating the management of teaching and learning activities. This brings positive benefits to the school, teachers, and students, reducing the pressures associated with record-keeping and documentation.

MVVM Model

MVVM stands for Model-View-ViewModel, a pattern used in building highly efficient and maintainable user interfaces (UI). This model divides the application into three main parts.

Overall functionality

Teachers - Individuals who manage the class, handle grade records, and assess students across various subjects.
Homeroom Teachers - The primary in-charge of the class, responsible for managing students, assessing conduct, and providing feedback on students' learning and training progress in the report cards.
School Administrators - Entities that oversee the entire scope and activities within the school, responsible for managing, distributing classes, and allocating teachers and students.
Administrators - Individuals who perform administrative roles, having access to all system functions. They are responsible for monitoring, upgrading, operating, and configuring the system.
Students - The attendees of the school and classes, who are the subjects of learning outcomes and assessments during semesters and school years.

Based on the above system agents, I have analyzed and listed the activities of each agent that can be reflected in the system:

Activities associated with Teachers:
- Manage students in the class.
- Manage subject grades.
- Manage class attendance.

Activities associated with Homeroom Teachers:
- Manage students in the class.
- Manage student report cards.
- Manage class attendance status.
- Manage subject grades.

Activities associated with School Administrators:
- Manage students in the school.
- Manage teachers in the school.
- Manage academic years and semesters.
- Manage subjects.
- Manage classes.

Technology

Restful API is a popular standard applied in designing APIs for web applications, aiming to optimize resource management. Instead of focusing on specifying how applications should behave, Restful API concentrates on managing system resources such as text files, images, audio, video, and dynamic data by formatting and transmitting their states through the HTTP protocol.

Security

JWT (JSON Web Token) is an open standard used to create and transmit authentication information securely and reliably between parties. JWTs are commonly used for user authentication and access authorization in web applications and web services. Key features of JWT include mobility and convenience, allowing for easy transmission of authentication information between parties without the need for storage in databases or stateful memory.
